Hi, I'm Filippo Di-Carlo. I'm a Graphic, Motion and Type Designer. I study at the Albertina Academy of Fine Arts in Turin, where I delve into visual design, motion graphics and typography to create creative and innovative projects.

My latest work [in progress] is Caractera, a variable old-style typeface designed to combine typographic tradition with modern flexibility.

A similar project is Egyptian Didone, a variable typeface developed for my graduate research: it has two axes of variation that allow for the hybridization of two different typographic styles.
